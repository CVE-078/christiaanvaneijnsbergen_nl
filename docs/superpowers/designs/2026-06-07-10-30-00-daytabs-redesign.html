Weekday strip redesign

Today each of the 7 tiles stacks day + focus + progress bar + count, so at 375px every tile is ~44px wide and reads crammed. Two ways out. A moves the verbose detail to a single summary line for the selected day and reduces each tile to day + a progress ring (fills, then a check when done; rest days are a quiet dash). B keeps the per-day focus label but trims each tile to day + focus + one ring, dropping the separate bar and number.
